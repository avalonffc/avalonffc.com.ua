Transportation of goods in containers

Avalon FFC performs container shipping between all ports of Europe, Asia, America, Africa and Australia.

The most popular directions of cargo transportation, today, for us are the various container terminals of the ports of China, India, Korea, Japan, Turkey, the USA, United Arab Emirates. Our company offers export transportation in the same areas as well.

In Ukraine, the work is organized through the port of Odessa and the sea transport port in Ilyichevsk.

Sea freight.

Urgent delivery of goods by air

Air delivery is optimal for delivery of goods from the United States, China, Australia and other countries. If you need to deliver the goods in the shortest possible time, the most suitable transport is aviation.

This service is not cheap, and is ideal for delivery of goods which are perishable, valuable and require urgent delivery.

Cold logistics

The company Avalon FFC offers its customers a range of services for the international transportation of goods that require compliance with a certain temperature, humidity and ventilation conditions during the entire transportation. As well as requiring special instructions for packing, loading and transportation.
 
Such goods are divided into the following categories depending on the origin:
 
Loads of plant origin: fruits, berries, vegetables, mushrooms, etc.
Cargo of animal origin: meat of various animals and birds, fish, caviar, milk, eggs, etc.
Processing products: sour cream, cottage cheese, fats, cheeses, frozen vegetables and fruits, sausages and other meat products, etc.
Living plants: seedlings, flowers, etc.
Delivery of such goods is possible by auto and air transport.
 
For auto transportation of the “Fresh” category of goods, are used cars with an isothermal body, refrigerators and “glaciers”. Avalon FFC works with refrigerators of various capacity, which allows to deliver different batches of goods.
 
Along with the organization of the delivery of goods Avalon FFC offers additional services for the consolidation and storage of goods in refrigerated warehouses in Europe.
